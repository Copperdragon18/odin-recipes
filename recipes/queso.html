Slow cooker cheese dip

Description

This chessy, creamy dip works for work potlucks and game day. As a slow cooker dish this dip is easy to make with little clean up.

    Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Ground beef
  • 12oz Velveta
  • 1 can Rotel tomatoes and chillies
  • 1 Pkg Taco Seasoning

    Steps

  1. Brown your ground beef.
  2. Cut up your Velveta into 1 inch squares.
  3. Add your ground beef, Rotel, Velveta, Taco seasoning to a slow cooker.
  4. Set to low for 6 hours or 3 hours on high. Stir occasionally until cheese melts and all Ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  5. Serve with your favorite chips.
