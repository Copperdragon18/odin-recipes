Burger

Discription

This delicious burger is a classic that no one can resist. This recipe will have you coming back for more.

    Ingredients

  • Groung beef
  • Bun
  • Lettus
  • Tomato
  • Pickels
  • Onion
  • Condiments

    Steps

  1. Form ground beef in to patty
  2. Season to taste and fry in a pan till brown
  3. Place cooked patty on bottom bun
  4. Top patty with Tomato, Onion, Lettus, Pickels, and Condiments.
