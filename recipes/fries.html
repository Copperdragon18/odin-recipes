Best Fries Ever

Description

This delicious french fry recipe will have you coming back for more. You will never go back to fast food fries again.

    Ingredients

  • 3 Potatoes
  • Peanut Oil
  • salt (to tastes)

    Steps

  1. Peel Potatoes
  2. Cut to into even rectangles
  3. soak in water for 30mins and up to 4hrs
  4. with 10 mins left prepare oil for frying
  5. heat oil to 325 degrees F.
  6. Dry off potatoes
  7. fry in oil for 5 to 7 mins or untill light brown and allow to drain and cool
  8. Once all are par cooked raise oil temp to 350 degrees F
  9. Cook potatoes again for 2 mins or untill golden brown.
  10. Toss with salt while still hot then allow to cool and enjoy.
